Solemate

A music app for runners

Solemate is a mobile application designed to enhance the running experience through music. Our feed-forward algorithm sets the runner's pace by playing music that varies in tempo. By encouraging the user to match their steps to the beat, our application cultivates a run that feels natural and inspires intrinsic motivation, especially for the beginner runner.

how it works

Runners are encouraged to match the pace of their running with the beat of the music (i.e., if the song has a BPM of 150, the runner's pace must be approximately the same), resulting in an intrinsic and natural running experience. The music will hint the user to walk or run based on the beats per minute (BPM) of the song. As users progress through levels, the duration of the run and the time spent running vs. walking increases. Progress is monitored and presented in the form of data visualization, allowing the user to view their accuracy in matching the beat of the music.

Solemate was proudly built with React Native, Firebase, Apple CoreMotion, and the Spotify SDK.
