Demo

Square Reader

The purpose of this project was to have a little fun with the Square ReaderSDK and Square Reader for magstripe. The Square Reader for magstripe allows anyone to take payments on their smartphone or tablet, either (1) with the Square POS app, or (2) integrating the readerSDK as I have done. I can then view transactions and other additional info on my Square seller dashboard.

Challenges & Next Steps

This was my first time working with Swift which had a bit of a learning curve but I managed to find useful tutorials online. I built the app with SwiftUI but was required to use UIKit for integrating Square's ReaderSDK. This posed the biggest challenge in my project as I had to use the UIViewRepresentable protocol to integrate my UIKit view into SwiftUI.

Though this was built for a fictional coffee shop, I built this app such that the menu can be adjusted to accomodate any product or service.
