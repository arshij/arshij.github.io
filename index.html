ABOUT ME

Hi there! I am an associate software engineer @ Moody's Analytics, building and learning at the intersection of finance and technology through the MATR (Moody's Analytics Technology Rotation) 2-year rotational program. I am located in the San Francisco Bay Area. I graduated in June of 2019 from Santa Clara University, earning my B.S. in Computer Science & Engineering and Minor in Mathematics. Go broncos!

Aside from developing and innovating, I have been an avid lover of racket sports and playing the piano from a very young age. I am extremely passionate about music. Check out my favorite podcast! I listen to Kanye, Kendrick and Drake on repeat. I enjoy reading books on philosophy and self-help. Streetwear, wellness and health are a big part of my lifestyle. Also, go Warriors!

Something I'm very proud of building is Solemate. I'd like to redesign and reimplement some features and take the product further - if that interests you, let's chat.
